Mycocell

Thesis, 2024

The Mycocell project merges nature and technology into a living, ephemeral, and sustainable architecture.
By combining mycelium, wool, and carbon fiber, a biohybrid material is created that dynamically responds to the environment. Born from morphogenesis and parametric design, Mycocell breathes with the earth, adapting to its surroundings and transforming each time.
At the Prato delle Cornacchie, it will bloom like a temporary dream - a light refuge shaped by wind and light - leaving a gentle trace before dissolving, deeply rooted in the cycles of life and the future.

Horizonte

Architecture competition, 2025

Horizonte begins with a white, flat roof seamlessly aligned with the road, framing the Paraná River’s panorama. A panoramic pool and a tapered structure guide the gaze outward, while a central staircase slices boldly to the residence below.
Inside, adobe walls contrast with the cool roof and glazed views, fostering warmth and openness. Curved forms anchor the space, directing movement and unveiling the river. Terraced courtyards and robust walls blend the home into the landscape, while a dramatic staircase connects it to the shoreline.
Horizonte celebrates contrasts, harmonizing architecture with nature in a poetic embrace of the horizon.

Cloud

Personal project, 2025

The Cloud Pavilion explores fluid geometry and parametric design, resembling a lightweight, floating volume.
Its undulating forms are composed of interconnected rings, creating a dynamic interplay of flowing surfaces. The structure filters light through its translucent material, generating a soft, diffused glow that enhances its ethereal presence.
Suspended above the ground, the pavilion appears weightless, blending organic curvature with precise computational design. Its organic shapes invite a sense of movement, transforming space into an architectural statement of lightness and adaptability.

Meadow of light

University project, 2023

The "Meadow of Light" kindergarten is a sanctuary of play and discovery, where architecture merges with nature.
Its design flows with openness, defined by delicate structures and vast glazed walls that dissolve the boundary between interior and landscape. A roof that seems to float brings an ethereal lightness, casting gentle rays through a luminous horizontal gap.
The interplay of solid and void shapes dynamic spaces, where children move freely between light-filled classrooms and green courtyards. Warm wood floors and soft hues create a sense of comfort, while rhythmic stone enclosures ground the design in timeless harmony. It is a place where light, space, and nature converge in serene dialogue.

Stone echoes

University project, 2022

"Stone Echoes" is a cultural symphony where architecture tells a story of continuity and renewal.
Anchored in the historic core of Bientina, the complex reinterprets the town’s lost volumes with a contemporary sensibility. The interplay of mass and void defines the design - light filters through apertures, tracing patterns of history and renewal. Interconnected plazas serve as thresholds, inviting exploration, while courtyards and paths align with ancient axes, weaving connections with the church and the surrounding urban fabric.
The architecture is both anchor and passage, where the past resounds and the present finds its voice in a harmonious dialogue of stone, light, and space.

The Veil

University project, 2022

"The Veil" enhances Pier Luigi Nervi’s iconic stadium, highlighting its architectural beauty.
The transparent roof allows light to permeate, creating a delicate veil that reveals the stadium's concrete structure from the outside while framing the breathtaking view of the Fiesole hills from within. The design emphasizes the harmony between Nervi’s masterful engineering and the natural landscape, making the roof both a protective cover and a window into the surrounding environment.

Nodo

Architecture competition, 2023

"Nodo" reimagines Piazza Italia, the heart of Maniago, as a timeless space of connection.
The design weaves together the city's history and modern life, creating a fluid, open expanse where paths converge and flow into each other. Its paved surface binds together key architectural elements - statues, fountains, and buildings - creating a unified whole. The materiality of stone and subtle gradations of levels guide movement, while strategic openings frame views of the surrounding landscape.
"Nodo" is both a passage and a meeting point, where the past and present are stitched together in a harmonious architectural thread.

Gates of Paradise

University project, 2022

The seminar "Abitare il paesaggio della storia" explores Ghiberti's Gates of Paradise through digital modeling, with the aim of deciphering the cultural value of the panels.
By studying the lands and landscapes that may have inspired Ghiberti, the project provides a deep reading of his scenes, enriching them with symbolic and historical analysis.
The result is a narrated video that, through 3D modeling and storytelling, makes the content of the panels tangible, transforming the artwork into a cultural experience that goes beyond aesthetics, revealing its profound meaning.

Zen house

Archviz, 2024

This architectural visualization presents a serene and contemplative interpretation of Zen House, designed by architect Jan Tyrpekl. Through detailed 3D modeling and rendering, the project captures the essence of minimalist design and its harmonious relationship with nature.
The rendered scenes explore the interplay between light, space, and materials, reflecting the tranquil atmosphere envisioned by the architect. The visualization emphasizes the meditative qualities of the house, from its clean lines to the subtle integration of outdoor elements, offering a visual experience of architectural serenity.

Incontro

Design competition, 2022
Team: Davide Di Bella, Petrina Razmovska

Incontro is a table that combines strength and lightness, presence and discretion. Four support points, two of which meet at the center, gently hold a thin surface in an embrace. The red oak wood, carefully treated, reveals its beauty through natural veins and soft forms. The rounded edges of the top gently touch the arch, creating the illusion of perfect balance.
Essential yet powerful, Incontro is the heart of every home, a place of exchange that warms and welcomes with every curve and every shade of wood.

Bobo

Design competition, 2023
Team: Davide Di Bella, Petrina Razmovska

Bobo is a playful collection of tables that infuses any space with a touch of fun and imagination.
Its whimsical, slightly anthropomorphic design features a single round steel top supported by thick cylindrical legs, each capped with curved tubular rings resembling small feet.
The result is a lighthearted and charming piece that adds character to any home. With its delightful form, Bobo is a joyful and endearing addition to living spaces, blending simplicity and humor in a design that feels both fresh and inviting.

Tsukiyo

Personal project, 2022
Team: Davide Di Bella, Petrina Razmovska

Tsukiyo is an NFT collection that merges generative visual art and haiku to foster a deep connection with nature. Each piece captures moments of quiet beauty, where landscapes, elements, and creatures exist in peaceful harmony. Both the artwork and haikus are generative, crafted with simplicity in mind, using calm, natural colors.
Together, image and verse tell stories of nature’s fleeting yet timeless essence. It invites the creation of narratives, pairing simple images with haiku, allowing each piece to evoke a unique, reflective experience of the natural world.
